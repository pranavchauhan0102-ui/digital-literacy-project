# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist

## General Online Safety
1. Use strong passwords with a mix of letters, numbers 
   and symbols — never use your name or birthday speciallyl.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all 
   important accounts like email and social media.
3. Never click on suspicious links received via email, 
   SMS or WhatsApp.
4. Always verify the identity of anyone asking for 
   personal or financial information online.
5. Keep your phone and apps updated to get the latest 
   security patches,it helps a lot.
6. Never share OTPs, passwords or PINs with anyone — 
   even if they claim to be from your bank because they never asks you such things. 

## UPI & Financial Safety
7. Remember — receiving money on UPI never requires 
   entering your PIN. If asked, it is a scam.
8. Always double check the UPI ID before sending money 
   — even to known contacts.

## How to Report Cybercrime
- Visit the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the 24x7 helpline: 1930.
- Report immediately — the faster you report, the better 
  the chance of recovering lost money.
