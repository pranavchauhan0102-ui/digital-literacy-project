# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
1. Keep your profile professional and up to date.
2. Think before you post — consider how it reflects on you.
3. Use privacy settings to control who sees your content.
4. Engage respectfully in online discussions and respect other's dignity too.
5. Share your academic achievements and projects.

## Don'ts
1. Don't share personal information like phone number publicly.
2. Don't post offensive or inappropriate content as it can effect your academic and personal life.
3. Don't engage in cyberbullying or online harassment.
4. Don't share confidential information about your college.
5. Don't spread unverified news or rumours online as it can have serious consequences.
